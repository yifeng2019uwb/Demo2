Preparing for your Upcoming Hiring Day
We’re excited to have you join us for our upcoming Interview Day! To help you prepare and manage your energy, here is a breakdown of the session flow and the technical expectations.
The Schedule
Technical Build (3 Hours): You’ll dive into a coding challenge to build a functional service from scratch.
Architecture & Decision Review (30 Mins): You’ll walk us through your code, the trade-offs you made, and the "why" behind your technical choices.
Break (15 Mins)
Behavioral Interview (45 Mins): A conversation focused on your past experiences, collaboration style, and alignment with DigitalOcean’s values.
Technical Details & Environment
The Challenge: You will be asked to build a functional REST API service that handles data ingestion and processing. We provide the requirements; you provide the engineering.
Languages: Our teams primarily use Go and Python, so we prefer to see solutions in one of those. However, if you are most comfortable in another language, you are welcome to use the language of your choice.
Hardware & AI: We will provide a laptop for the session with GitHub Copilot enabled. You are also welcome to use any additional AI tools or documentation that help you work efficiently.
Deployment: This is a full-cycle exercise. You will be provided with DigitalOcean cloud credits and will be expected to deploy your service to our platform during the 3-hour window.
What We’re Looking For We aren't just looking for a "working" script; we want to see "production-ready" thinking. Our evaluation focuses on:
Engineering Quality: Clean code organization, sensible validation, and meaningful error handling.
Testing: Evidence of a structured approach to verifying code through automated testing and validation.
Automation & Workflow: Ability to leverage automation for consistent delivery and a streamlined development lifecycle.
Operational Excellence: Consideration for how the service functions in a live environment, including observability, configurability, and scalability.
The Review & Behavioral Session After the build, you’ll have 30 minutes to discuss your architectural trade-offs—what you prioritized, what you skipped, and how you would scale the service. We’ll then wrap up with a behavioral interview to learn more about how you solve problems and work within a team.
We’re looking forward to a great day of building!
